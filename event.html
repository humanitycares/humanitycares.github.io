🛠️ Skill Development Workshop

Humanity Cares conducts Skill Development Workshops to empower individuals with practical knowledge and hands-on training in various fields such as tailoring, computer literacy, handicrafts, and more. These workshops aim to enhance employability, encourage self-reliance, and open up new livelihood opportunities for youth and women from underprivileged backgrounds. 🛒 Monthly Ration Supply

Humanity Cares runs a Monthly Ration Supply Program to support underprivileged families struggling to meet basic needs. Every month, we distribute essential groceries including rice, wheat, pulses, oil, and other daily necessities to ensure that no family goes to bed hungry. This ongoing initiative aims to bring food security, dignity, and relief to those facing financial hardships, especially widows, the elderly, and daily wage earners. Volunteers personally reach out to identify the most deserving beneficiaries in urban slums and rural areas.
