Doha Music Lovers

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
Doha Music Lovers is a vibrant community in Qatar dedicated to uniting people through the universal language of music. Founded with the vision of bringing together musicians, singers, and music enthusiasts from diverse cultures, the group has become a hub for creativity, collaboration, and cultural exchange. Over the years, Doha Music Lovers has organized live performances, charity concerts, and social gatherings, providing a platform for both emerging and established artists to showcase their talent. Beyond entertainment, the group believes in using music as a tool for goodwill, fostering friendships, and supporting charitable causes within the community.
To celebrate and promote the joy of music by bringing together musicians, singers, and enthusiasts from all walks of life. Doha Music Lovers is committed to fostering cultural harmony, nurturing talent, and creating memorable musical experiences that inspire, connect, and uplift the community..
To be one of the most vibrant and inclusive music community, where talent thrives, cultures unite, and music becomes a bridge that connects hearts across the world.

Doha Music Lovers - Anniversary Celebrations

Doha Music Lovers Group, a well-known musical group in Qatar, recently celebrated its first anniversary at the Integrated Indian Community Centre (IICC), which was attended by many intellectuals, delegates and music-loving personalities.

Super Dancer Doha - Season 2

"Super Dancer Doha Season 2" refers to the second season of the "Super Dancer Dance Competition" organized by Doha Music Lovers in association with Emote Edition Dance Studios. The competition, which aimed to provide a platform for talented dancers in Doha, concluded successfully with Avanthika Rajesh winning the solo category and the Nrutyodaya team winning the group category.

Super Dancer Season 3 contest concludes

The Super Dancer Competition (Season 3), organised by Doha Music Lovers in association with Emote Edition Dance Studio, concluded with a grand finale. Over three weeks, hundreds of dancers competed in the auditions, in classical, folk, cinematic, and western dance forms. From this talent pool, 80 participants were selected to perform in the finale, competing across four categories..
