Frequently Asked Questions

What is Humanity Cares?

Humanity Cares is a non-profit organization committed to supporting education, healthcare, food distribution, and social welfare programs for the underprivileged.

How can I donate?

You can donate online through our secure donation portal or via bank transfer. Visit our donation page for details.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations made to Humanity Cares are eligible for tax benefits under applicable laws.

Can I volunteer?

Absolutely! Fill out the volunteer form on our volunteer page and our team will reach out to you.

Where does my donation go?

Your donation directly supports our initiatives like food distribution, medical aid, scholarships, and community support projects.
